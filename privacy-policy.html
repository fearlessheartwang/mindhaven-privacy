欢迎使用心岛 MindHaven（以下简称"我们"或"本应用"）。我们非常重视您的隐私保护和个人信息安全。本隐私政策将帮助您了解我们如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

1.1 您主动提供的信息

  • 账户信息：邮箱地址、昵称
  • 冥想记录：冥想时长、频率、场景选择
  • 社区内容：分享的文字、评论、点赞记录
  • 好友关系：添加的好友列表
  • 个性签名：您设置的个性签名和个人简介

1.2 自动收集的信息

  • 设备信息：设备型号、操作系统版本
  • 使用数据：应用使用时长、功能使用频率
  • 日志信息：错误日志、性能数据

2. 信息的使用

我们使用收集的信息用于：

  • 提供和改进应用服务
  • 同步您的冥想数据到云端
  • 生成个性化的统计和建议
  • 维护社区安全和秩序
  • 修复bug和优化性能
  • 发送您订阅的冥想提醒通知

3. 信息的存储

  • 数据存储在 Supabase 云服务器（位于美国）
  • 所有数据传输使用 HTTPS 加密
  • 密码使用行业标准的哈希算法加密存储
  • 我们会保留您的数据直到您删除账户
  • 账户删除后，您的数据将在30天内永久删除

4. 第三方服务

本应用使用以下第三方服务：

  • Supabase：用于数据存储、用户认证和实时同步
  • Web Audio API：用于生成冥想音景（完全在本地运行，不涉及数据传输）

这些第三方服务提供商可能会根据其自己的隐私政策处理您的信息。我们建议您查看他们的隐私政策以了解详情。

5. 信息的分享

我们承诺：

  • 不会出售您的个人信息
  • 不会向第三方分享您的信息，除非：
    • 获得您的明确同意
    • 法律法规要求
    • 保护用户或公众的安全
  • 社区内容（分享、评论）将对其他用户可见

6. 您的权利

您有权：

  • 访问和导出您的数据
  • 修改您的个人信息
  • 删除您的账户和所有数据
  • 拒绝某些数据的收集
  • 撤回同意
  • 控制通知权限和提醒设置

如何删除账户：您可以通过"个人中心" → "退出登录" → "删除账户"来永久删除您的账户和所有相关数据。

7. 通知权限

如果您启用了每日冥想提醒功能，我们会请求通知权限以在您设定的时间发送提醒。您可以随时在应用设置中启用或关闭此功能。通知完全在本地生成，不会收集或传输任何额外数据。

8. 儿童隐私

本应用面向13岁及以上用户。我们不会故意收集13岁以下儿童的个人信息。如果您发现我们无意中收集了儿童信息，请联系我们，我们将立即删除。

9. Cookie和追踪技术

我们使用本地存储（LocalStorage）来保存您的偏好设置和会话信息。这些信息仅存储在您的设备上，不会被发送到服务器。我们不使用Cookie或第三方追踪技术。

10. 数据安全

我们采取以下措施保护您的数据：

  • 使用 SSL/TLS 加密传输
  • 密码哈希存储
  • 定期安全审计
  • 访问控制和权限管理
  • 数据备份和灾难恢复

尽管我们采取了合理的安全措施，但请注意，没有任何系统是100%安全的。

11. 数据保留和删除

  • 活跃账户：您的数据将一直保留，直到您选择删除账户
  • 账户删除后：您的所有数据（包括冥想记录、社区内容、好友关系）将在30天内永久删除
  • 备份数据：出于数据安全考虑，备份数据可能会保留额外90天，之后也将永久删除

12. 隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。重大变更时，我们会通过应用内通知或邮件告知您。继续使用应用即表示您同意更新后的隐私政策。

13. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何问题或建议，或希望行使您的数据权利，请通过以下方式联系我们：

邮箱：fearless.heart.wang@gmail.com

我们将在收到您的请求后30天内回复。

Welcome to MindHaven (referred to as "we," "our," or "the app"). We take your privacy and personal information security very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, nickname
  • Meditation Records: Duration, frequency, scenario choices
  • Community Content: Posts, comments, likes
  • Friend Relationships: Your friends list
  • Bio: Your personal signature and bio

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App usage duration, feature usage frequency
  • Log Information: Error logs, performance data

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve app services
  • Sync your meditation data to the cloud
  • Generate personalized statistics and recommendations
  • Maintain community safety and order
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance
  • Send meditation reminder notifications you subscribe to

3. Information Storage

  • Data is stored on Supabase cloud servers (located in the United States)
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • We retain your data until you delete your account
  • After account deletion, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For data storage, user authentication, and real-time sync
  • Web Audio API: For generating meditation soundscapes (runs entirely locally, no data transmission)

These third-party service providers may process your information according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for details.

5. Information Sharing

We promise:

  • We will not sell your personal information
  • We will not share your information with third parties, except:
    • With your explicit consent
    • As required by law or regulation
    • To protect user or public safety
  • Community content (posts, comments) will be visible to other users

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data
  • Modify your personal information
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Refuse certain data collection
  • Withdraw consent
  • Control notification permissions and reminder settings

How to delete your account: You can permanently delete your account and all related data by going to "Profile" → "Sign Out" → "Delete Account".

7. Notification Permissions

If you enable the daily meditation reminder feature, we will request notification permission to send reminders at your set time. You can enable or disable this feature anytime in app settings. Notifications are generated entirely locally and do not collect or transmit any additional data.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use local storage (LocalStorage) to save your preferences and session information. This information is stored only on your device and is not sent to servers. We do not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies.

10. Data Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encrypted transmission
  • Password hash storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Access control and permission management
  • Data backup and disaster recovery

Although we take reasonable security measures, please note that no system is 100% secure.

11. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Active Accounts: Your data will be retained until you choose to delete your account
  • After Account Deletion: All your data (including meditation records, community content, friend relationships) will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup Data: For data security purposes, backup data may be retained for an additional 90 days, after which it will also be permanently deleted

12. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or email. Continued use of the app indicates your agreement to the updated Privacy Policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us:

Email: fearless.heart.wang@gmail.com

We will respond to your request within 30 days of receipt.
